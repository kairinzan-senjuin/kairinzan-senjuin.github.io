About Senjuin

About

Kairinzan Senjuin is a Koyasan Shingon Buddhist temple located in Sora, Anamizu, Hosu District, Ishikawa Prefecture. Situated in a scenic location overlooking a tranquil bay, it has long been cherished by the local community.

The temple was damaged in the 2024 Noto Peninsula Earthquake, but is walking the path of recovery together with the support of the local community. Step away from the noise of everyday life and spend a special time in reflection.

Through various experiences such as sutra copying, chanting, and shojin ryori (Buddhist cuisine), you can connect with the teachings of Buddhism.
