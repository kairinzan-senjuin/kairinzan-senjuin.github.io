A Time for Noh and Prayer

Senjuin holds Noh prayer ceremonies and workshops. The sacred Noh performance in the main hall by professional Noh actors is a special time to offer prayers for Noto's recovery and peace through the solemn dance and chant of Noh.

In the workshop, you can learn the basic movements of Noh directly from a professional Noh actor. Beginners are welcome to participate.

Program

1

Noh Dedication & Prayer

A sacred Noh performance in the main hall by a professional Noh actor. Prayers for Noto's recovery and peace are offered through the solemn dance and chant of Noh.

2

Noh Movement Workshop

A hands-on workshop where you can learn basic Noh movements (sliding walk, stance, and fan handling) directly from a professional Noh actor.

3

Noh Masks & Costumes Explained

An explanation of the meaning and history of Noh masks and costumes. A rare opportunity to see and touch them up close.
